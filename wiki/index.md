## Concepts

- [[cosmic-rays]] - Relativistic charged particles whose propagation and coupling to plasma can affect astrophysical gas dynamics.
- [[cosmic-ray-feedback]] - The role of cosmic rays in heating, pressure support, winds, star formation regulation, and cluster cooling.
- [[cosmic-ray-transport]] - Diffusion, streaming, scattering, and spectral evolution of cosmic rays through magnetized plasma.
- [[galactic-winds]] - Large-scale gas outflows that cosmic ray pressure gradients may help launch or regulate.
- [[circumgalactic-medium]] - Gas around galaxies whose pressure, temperature, and observables may be altered by cosmic rays.
- [[agn-feedback]] - Feedback from active galactic nuclei, including possible cosmic ray heating of cluster gas.
