## Concepts

- [[astrophysics]] - Broad research scope for the wiki, currently seeded by cosmic ray feedback.
- [[multiphase-ism]] - Central physical setting connecting star formation, feedback, gas phases, winds, and observables.
- [[tigress]] - Simulation framework for resolved multiphase ISM, star formation, and feedback studies.
- [[stellar-feedback]] - Energy, momentum, radiation, mass, and metals injected by young and massive stars.
- [[star-formation-regulation]] - Processes that balance gravity, pressure, turbulence, magnetic fields, and feedback to set star formation rates.
- [[supernova-feedback]] - Mechanical feedback from supernova remnants, superbubbles, and clustered explosions.
- [[observational-ism-diagnostics]] - H I, CO, dust, radio, and Local Group survey diagnostics of ISM structure.
- [[cosmic-rays]] - Relativistic charged particles whose propagation and coupling to plasma can affect astrophysical gas dynamics.
- [[cosmic-ray-feedback]] - The role of cosmic rays in heating, pressure support, winds, star formation regulation, and cluster cooling.
- [[cosmic-ray-transport]] - Diffusion, streaming, scattering, and spectral evolution of cosmic rays through magnetized plasma.
- [[galactic-winds]] - Large-scale gas outflows that cosmic ray pressure gradients may help launch or regulate.
- [[circumgalactic-medium]] - Gas around galaxies whose pressure, temperature, and observables may be altered by cosmic rays.
- [[agn-feedback]] - Feedback from active galactic nuclei, including possible cosmic ray heating of cluster gas.
