# TIGRESS

## Definition

TIGRESS is a simulation framework for studying the three-phase interstellar medium, star formation, and stellar feedback in galactic environments.

## Why It Matters

TIGRESS and related TIGRESS-NCR work form a backbone of the publication corpus. The framework links local multiphase ISM physics to star formation regulation, synthetic observables, galactic winds, spiral structure, nuclear rings, and environmental effects.
