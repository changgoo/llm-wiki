# Supernova Feedback

## Definition

Supernova feedback is the transfer of energy, momentum, mass, and metals from supernova explosions into the surrounding interstellar medium.

## Why It Matters

The bibliography contains a dedicated cluster on supernova remnants, momentum injection, superbubbles, cloudy multiphase media, and the loading of galactic winds. This topic anchors the mechanical feedback side of the wiki.

## Supporting Evidence

- [[publication-ingest-plan]] places supernovae, bubbles, and feedback coupling in the second full-text batch.

- [[2017-kim-ostriker-tigress-framework]] implements supernova feedback with resolved energy injection when possible and terminal momentum injection otherwise.
