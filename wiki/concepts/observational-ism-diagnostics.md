# Observational ISM Diagnostics

## Definition

Observational ISM diagnostics are measurements used to infer gas phase structure, kinematics, magnetic fields, chemistry, and star formation conditions in the interstellar medium.

## Why It Matters

The bibliography includes a substantial observation-facing cluster: H I absorption and emission, cold neutral medium measurements, CO conversion factors, dust polarization, radio continuum, and Local Group surveys. These papers connect simulation predictions to measurable quantities.
