# Astrophysics

## Definition

Astrophysics is the study of the physical processes governing astronomical objects and systems, from plasma processes and stellar feedback to galaxies, clusters, and cosmological structure formation.

## Why It Matters

This wiki is scoped as an astrophysics research knowledge base. [[cosmic-ray-feedback]] is the first seeded topic, but future sources may cover other areas of astrophysics as long as they contribute to the broader research context.
