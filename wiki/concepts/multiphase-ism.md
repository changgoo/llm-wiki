# Multiphase ISM

## Definition

The multiphase interstellar medium is gas organized into coexisting cold, warm, hot, neutral, ionized, atomic, and molecular phases shaped by gravity, turbulence, magnetic fields, heating, cooling, chemistry, and stellar feedback.

## Why It Matters

The cleaned publication corpus uses the multiphase ISM as its central physical setting. It connects local star formation regulation, supernova remnants, superbubbles, galactic winds, H I and CO diagnostics, and galaxy-scale feedback.
