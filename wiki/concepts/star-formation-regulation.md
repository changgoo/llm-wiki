# Star Formation Regulation

## Definition

Star formation regulation is the set of processes that determine how gas turns into stars by balancing gravity, turbulence, thermal pressure, magnetic fields, radiation, chemistry, and feedback.

## Why It Matters

Many papers in the bibliography study pressure-regulated and feedback-modulated star formation in galactic disks and its transfer into larger-scale galaxy formation models.
