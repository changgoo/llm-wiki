# My Papers Bibliography

## Summary

`raw/sources/mypapers.bib` is a cleaned bibliography of 72 astrophysics publications. The entries include titles, authors, publication metadata, keywords, abstracts for most records, DOIs, ADS URLs, and arXiv identifiers when available.

The bibliography is sufficient for metadata-level organization before full PDFs are available. It supports a first-pass topic taxonomy, publication timeline, and ingest plan. Full source ingests should wait for PDF paths and generated `.txt` companions.
