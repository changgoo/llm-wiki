# Kim And Basu 2013 Decaying MHD Turbulence

## Summary

This paper studies decaying magnetohydrodynamic turbulence in cold neutral medium sheets embedded in warm neutral medium. Unlike local isothermal turbulence boxes, the simulations include interstellar cooling and heating and an explicit multiphase geometry.

## Key Claims

- Early turbulent kinetic energy decay resembles standard compressible MHD turbulence, with rapid power-law decay.
- Strong magnetic fields perpendicular to cold sheets can leave long-lived incompressible motions in the sheet plane.
- Boundary conditions and Alfvén-wave leakage matter: isolated sheets decay rapidly, while periodic-like replenishment can sustain motions longer.
- Realistic global multiphase simulations are needed to determine whether this reduced decay occurs in the ISM.
