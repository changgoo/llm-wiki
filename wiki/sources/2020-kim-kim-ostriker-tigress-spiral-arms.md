# Kim, Kim, And Ostriker 2020 TIGRESS Spiral Arms

## Summary

This paper extends TIGRESS to include a stellar spiral-arm potential and studies how spiral arms affect star formation, magnetic fields, and arm substructures such as spurs and feathers.

## Key Claims

- Spiral arms concentrate more than 90% of star formation into arm regions, but enhance the global star formation rate only modestly.
- This supports the view that spiral arms organize star formation spatially rather than strongly triggering it globally.
- Clustered feedback in spiral arms creates superbubbles whose distorted boundaries become transient spurs or feathers.
- Local star formation rate surface density remains roughly tied to gas surface density and total midplane stress, consistent with pressure-regulated star formation.
